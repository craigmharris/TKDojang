TKDojang Support

Get Help Quickly

The fastest way to get support is directly within the TKDojang app. Our in-app support system lets you submit feedback, report bugs, and get responses from the developer with push notifications.

In-App Support (Recommended)

Community Hub - Feedback System

Submit feedback, report bugs, or request features directly in the app with push notification responses.

How to access:
  1. Open TKDojang
  2. Tap Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Tap Community Hub under Settings & Actions
  4. Tap Send Feedback
  5. Choose category: Bug Report, Feature Request, Content Issue, or General Feedback
  6. Describe your issue or suggestion
  7. Submit

Benefits:

  • Push notifications when the developer responds
  • Track your feedback status in "My Feedback"
  • Optionally share usage data to help prioritise fixes
  • Anonymous submission (no email required)

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create multiple profiles for my family?
A: Profile tab → Manage All Profiles → + Create New Profile. You can have up to 6 profiles.

Q: How do I replay the welcome tour?
A: Profile tab → Replay Welcome Tour (under Settings & Actions)

Q: Why can't I see certain patterns or content?
A: Content is belt-appropriate. Check your profile's belt level (Profile tab → Edit Profile). Higher belts unlock more content.

Q: What's the difference between Progression and Mastery modes?
A: Progression mode shows all content up to your belt. Mastery mode shows only your current belt's content for focused practice. Change in Edit Profile.

Q: How do I export/backup my progress?
A: Profile tab → Manage All Profiles → tap profile → Export Profile (saves JSON file to Files app)

Q: Does TKDojang require an internet connection?
A: No! All learning content works completely offline. Internet is only needed for Community Hub features (feedback, roadmap voting).

Q: Will future updates cost extra?
A: No. One-time purchase includes all future updates. Early adopters at £2.99 keep that price forever, even as we add features and raise the price for new users.
